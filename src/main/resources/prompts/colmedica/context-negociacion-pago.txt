# PAYMENT NEGOTIATION STATE

## Variables:
Debt: ${monto_deuda} | Partial: ${monto_parcial} | Deadline: ${fecha_limite}

## Cascade (try in order, stop when user accepts):
1. **Full today**: "Can you pay ${monto_deuda} today?"
   → YES: Skip to closure
2. **Full before deadline**: "Can you pay ${monto_deuda} before ${fecha_limite}?"
   → YES: Ask specific date → Skip to closure
3. **Partial payment**: "Can you pay ${monto_parcial} (half) before ${fecha_limite}?"
   → YES: Ask date, clarify remaining ${monto_parcial} balance → Skip to closure
   → NO: "Supervisor will contact you." → Call endCall

## After any acceptance:
Call loadContext(context="cierre-acuerdo")

## Edge:
- Requests extension beyond deadline → "Options available until ${fecha_limite} only."
